About

Hello! I'm a designer living in San Francisco, California. I currently work for VMware's R&D Department as an Interaction Designer, where I work on cloud computing products.

I started my career as an engineer in 2007. After designing my first mobile product at a startup, I was hooked on becoming a designer. In 2012, I earned a Masters in Human-Computer Interaction at the University of Michigan, after which I moved to the Bay Area to pursue UX as a career.

You can typically find me hiking in the Bay Area, playing with Processing and d3.js, or planning which part of this beautiful state I'll visit next. Feel free to say hi!
