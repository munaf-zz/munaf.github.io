Core Merchant

With two friends, I founded a payments company in Detroit. It now handles payments for an NBA team, a major finance company, and hundreds of others. I left in 2011 to do a Masters at the University of Michigan.
Beginnings

In 2008, Core began as an expense reporting software company. We were trying to solve a problem we had at our jobs: tracking business expenses without collecting receipts. I was the technology lead on this project.

Payment Processing

While studying payment technologies for expenses, we learned how hard it was for companies to accept credit cards at a constant transaction rate. After interviewing potential customers, we decided to focus on solving this problem for enterprises.

Core Today

The company is headquartered in Downtown Detroit, and has saved clients millions in transaction fees. In addition to providing payment services, Core is launching several products in 2014.
