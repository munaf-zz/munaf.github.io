Munaf Assaf

User Experience Designer

Core Merchant

In 2008, I co-founded a payment tech startup in Detroit with two friends. Without any design training, I led product design and engineering. I left in 2011 to do a Masters in HCI at the University of Michigan.
Beginnings

Core began as an expense reporting software company. We were trying to solve a problem we had at our jobs: tracking business expenses without collecting receipts. Our early designs targeted BlackBerry, then the dominant mobile platform for enterprises.

Payment Processing

While studying payment technologies for expenses, we learned how hard it was for companies to accept credit cards at a constant transaction rate. After interviewing potential customers, we decided to focus on solving this problem.

Core Today

The company is headquartered in Downtown Detroit, and has saved companies millions in transaction fees. Core handles payments for an NBA team, a major financial company, and hundreds of other clients. In addition to providing payment services, Core is launching several products in 2014.
